### **Selected Problem: Thermal Barrier Coatings (TBC) on Turbine Blades**
In this problem, we analyzed a **superalloy turbine blade** ($k_b = 25$ **W/mK**) exposed to hot gases at **1616 K**. We first calculated the surface temperature of the blade without any protection and found it reached **1236 K**, which exceeded the maximum allowable limit ($T_{max}$) of **1200 K**. We then re-evaluated the system after adding a thin **0.5 mm** **Thermal Barrier Coating** ($k_{TBC} = 1$ **W/mK**) and accounting for an interfacial thermal resistance of **$10^{-4} m^{2}$ K/W**.
